Spiritual Direction

Spiritual direction is the art of helping a person with his or her relationship with God. Spiritual direction proposes to help people relate personally to God, to let God relate personally to them, and to enable them to live within that relationship. The focus of spiritual direction is always the relationship between the person and God. Spiritual direction is confidential, compassionate, non-judgmental, and non-dogmatic. It proceeds at the pace the person finds comfortable.
